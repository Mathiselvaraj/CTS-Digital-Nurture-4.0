Help Center - Local Community Event Portal

How to Navigate the Portal

  • Use the navigation bar at the top to move between Home, Events, and Contact pages.
  • Click on Help to access this guide.

Registering for Events

  • Go to the Events section.
  • Fill out the registration form with your details.
  • Choose an event type from the dropdown to view the fee.
  • Click Submit to register. You’ll see a confirmation message.

Viewing Event Gallery

  • In the Events section, view images of past events.
  • Double-click on an image to enlarge it.

Using Geolocation

  • Click the "Find Nearby Events" button to fetch your current location.
  • Your coordinates will appear below the button.

Saving Preferences

  • Your selected event type is saved automatically in local storage.
  • To clear all saved preferences, click the "Clear Preferences" button in the form.
